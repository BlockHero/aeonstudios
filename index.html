More About Me

I'm Nayan and I make stuff... well I already said that on homepage, we gonna go slightly more in detail about things i do and why i do them. So let's start my mini self essay with hobbies and interests.

1. Art
Drawing stuff was probably my first hobby ever. I started drawing when i was very small. I like to draw cartoons or comic book characters mostly. I also had interest in making things out of waste items like paper or cardboard and more stuff like that. I would make my own paper-cardboard toys. I don't do those cardboard things anymore cuz lack of time and resources but i still draw sometimes.
2. YouTube
When i first got access to internet obviously first thing i did was downloading and playing lots of games, then right after that it was the youtube craze. I started making vids around 2016 but my actual main acc started in 2017 where i posted continuously. I gave up sometimes but always picked up after breaks. My biggest milestones for me were having 100k+ views on my channel without the help of YT shorts. and max sub were just 400 or something. I posted here about everything I knew and learnt in my life, so it was very important to me. Sadly in 2025 it got deleted when it was lowkey hacked so it all went away. I remade new channel few months later but it never got same success as before but it's not like I'll quit now. Having a yt channel led me into learning lots of new things so it's not like all of it was waste, i learn photo and video editing and some SEO stuff.

Like i mentioned above, I love playing games. even since i was a small baby, I started with dad's phone and computer and sum consoles, then as time progressed I used my own phone and then computer and emulators and what to not to play every game I wanted.
